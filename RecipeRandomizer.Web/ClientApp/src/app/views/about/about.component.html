About

The idea for this project came about during the 2020 pandemic lockdown, when I wanted to cook something but I didn't know what and I didn't want to rummage through all my cookbooks. I wanted a simple way to give me a random recipe. I found none that really fitted my needs, so I began coding my own randomizer application. Obvously I got carried away a "little" and it is now a full-blown web-app with database üòÖ

FAQ

Do I need an account to use this?

No you don't. All recipes are freely accessible to everyone.

Can I upload my own recipes?

Yes! You are even encouraged to do so, the more the merrier. Just create an account and you can begin sharing your recipes with the world and much more!

Is this repository moderated?

Yes to a certain degree. If you upload inappropriate content you can get blocked by an admin!

Do I have to pay for anything?

No. This project is free of charge for everyone.

I found an issue/somthing does not work

Please post any issues you come across here: issues tracker
